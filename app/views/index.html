Restoring Order and Control (ROC), includes a number of measures aimed at restoring fairness and public confidence in the asylum system.

The 'Safe and legal' stream will support vulnerable people in need of protection.

Community groups will sponsor vulnerable people to come to the UK. The Lead Sponsor will facilitate the initial training of community groups and complete suitability.

A sponsoring organisation will need to be accredited by the Home Office before they are able to sponsor a community group. Lead sponsors will be required to apply for accreditation by completing an online form.
