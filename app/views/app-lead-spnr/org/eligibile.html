Lead sponsor eligibility

To be eligible, your organisation must:

  • be a registered legal entity
  • be based and operate in the UK
  • have the capability, resources and networks to act as a lead sponsor
  • be able to support multiple sponsor groups
  • have a governance board where the majority of members are British citizens or Irish citizens

Your oganisation must also have:

  • a complaints procedure
  • data protection and safeguarding policies
