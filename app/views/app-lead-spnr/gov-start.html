Apply to be a lead sponsor

Use this form to apply to become a lead sponsor for the refugee sponsorship route. You can apply for the:

  • study route
  • name route

Applications for the work route are not open yet. They are expeced to open in 2027.

If you want to apply for multiple routes, you must complete a separate application form for each route.

Who can apply?

Registered charities and community interest companies (CICs) can apply to be a lead sponsor.

You cannot apply as an individual or sole trader.

Before you start

Make sure you have everything you need before starting the application form. You will not be able to save and return to it later.

Read the guidance

Read the lead sponsor operational guidance. The guidance explains what you need to provide in your application.

Gather the information you need

You will need to provide:

  • your organisation's name and address
  • your charity number or company number
  • information about your board members (full name, date of birth, nationality, passport number or UK driving licence number), email address
  • the names of any partner organisations, if you are applying as a consortium

You will also need to upload a copy of your:

  • annual accounts
  • safeguarding policy
  • complaints procedure

You cannot apply as an individual or sole trader.

Prepare your supporting document

You will need to prepare a document explaining how your organisation will deliver the role of a lead sponsor.

The lead sponsor operational guidance explains what information you need to include.
