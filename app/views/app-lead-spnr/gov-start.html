Lead sponsor eligibility

What is a lead sponsor?

A lead sponsor is a charity approved to suport community sponsor groups.

The role includes:

  • completing suitability checks on community sponsors
  • helping community sponsors prepare and submit applications
  • acting as the main point of contact between community sponsors and the Home Office
  • providing advice and support to community sponsors

Who can apply?

Only registered charities can apply to be a lead sponsor. You cannot apply as an individual or sole trader.

Before you start

You will need to provide:

  • your organisations' details, including name, address and website
  • your charity number
  • details of ket people, including name, date of birth and nationality

You will also need to upload a copy of your:

  • annual accounts
  • safeguarding policy
  • complaints procedure
